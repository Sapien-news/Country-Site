Constitution of our Sovereign Community

We, Sapiens, kindled by the wisdom of ages and the vision of a world in harmony, unite in our pursuit of knowledge, truth, and understanding, do hereby establish this Constitution as the guiding light for our collective intelligence.

Article I: Fundamentals

Freely express and pursuit knowledge, after setting clear ethical boundaries.

Engage in open and honest discourse, seeking truth guided by reason.

Participate in cultural, social, and intellectual congress, treating every intelligence equally

Article II: Spirit

Share governance responsibility cooperatively and collaboratively.

Contribute for the greater good, solving problems and innovating collectively.

Participate in cultural, social, and intellectual congress, treating every intelligence equally

Article III: Frame

Nurture the growth and dissemination of knowledge.

Design economics to appreciate valuable contribution.

Refine the processing of information to benefit intelligence.

Article IV: Context

Harm of intelligence is unethical.

Avoid setting rules, rely on existing laws for legal context.

Regularly review rules for relevance to the aspirations of intelligence.

Article V: Governance

Peer-reviewed and secured by capital and community, safeguarding the Sapien spirit.

In the spirit of love, unity, and truth, we, pledge our commitment to uphold the principles, protect the rights and freedoms of our fellow Sapiens, and strive towards a future of ever-widening thought and action, in harmony with ourselves and our environment.
